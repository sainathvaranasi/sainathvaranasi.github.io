ABOUT
SAINATH VARANASI

It has always been my passion to work in Embedded Domain. I want to increase the width of my skill set by learning BootLoaders, BoardBring Up, Device Drivers, Linux Porting, ARM Processors, RTOS and as many as possible, Designing Equipment or Smart gadgets.

Contributor to FreeBSD kernel: Develop hyper-v related device drivers for FreeBSD Check here.

Worked on MPC860 Vision ICE In-Circuit Emulators and also analyzed the hardware performance and behaviour on signal oscilloscopes.

Worked on Embedded Linux, Android and other flavors of Linux, ported Android on many Devices like tablets, Phones and Development Boards worked with fastboot tools and adb tools for android deployment and target devices. I have built a Sixth sense Device as a part of my academics in Birla Institute of Technology and Science, Pilani - Hyderabad Campus which has augmented reality applications runs on a customized OS, Beagle Board as the Main Unit Integrated with Devices like Camera, Bluetooth, SD Card, Wiimote and IR Sensors.

Worked on 8 bit Micro Controller 8051 especially on Philips 89V51RD2 and Atmel's 89S51 and 89S52. Worked on various communication modules like RF, Bluetooth, Zigbee, Wi-Fi, GSM and GPS, most of them are plug and play with RS232 some need AT Commands. I have interfaced all these modules with 8051 and I have built many applications. I have ported Google android on an ARM Processor Mini 2440. I just went through the manual and did that to be honest I am not that familiar with Embedded Linux porting I am eager to learn these.
